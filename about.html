About Omni Electrical Estimating

Omni Electrical Estimating provides professional electrical estimating support for contractors who need dependable bid capacity without adding full-time overhead.

We support commercial, industrial, tenant improvement, design-build, and service-based electrical projects through a flexible network of experienced estimators.

Our goal is simple: help electrical contractors bid more work, improve accuracy, and meet deadlines using the estimating software and workflow that best fits their business.

Built for Contractor Flexibility

Every contractor estimates differently. Omni Electrical Estimating is designed to work with client-preferred platforms including Electrical Bid Manager, Accubid, McCormick, ConEst, Bluebeam, and other estimating workflows.

Scalable Estimating Support

Whether a contractor needs one estimate, overflow estimating support, or ongoing bid assistance, Omni Electrical Estimating is built to provide skilled support when it is needed most.
