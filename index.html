Field-Based Estimating

Omni Electrical Estimating was founded by William Seitz, an electrical contractor, foreman, project manager, and estimator with experience across commercial, industrial, mixed-use, tenant improvement, and residential electrical construction.

With experience in estimating, project management, procurement, scheduling, field operations, Electrical Bid Manager, and bid preparation, Omni Electrical Estimating provides practical estimating support grounded in real construction knowledge.

Our mission is simple: help electrical contractors bid smarter, improve estimating accuracy, and reduce estimating overhead.
