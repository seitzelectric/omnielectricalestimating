Careers at Omni Electrical Estimating

Omni Electrical Estimating is building a professional team of skilled electrical estimators and preconstruction specialists.

We are interested in connecting with seasoned estimators, as well as up-and-coming professionals who are familiar with the latest estimating software, digital takeoff tools, and technology used in the electrical construction industry.

Who We Are Looking For

  • Experienced electrical estimators
  • Commercial and industrial estimating professionals
  • Preconstruction specialists
  • Electrical takeoff specialists
  • Professionals familiar with modern estimating software
  • Detail-oriented candidates who understand deadlines and accuracy

Preferred Software Experience

  • Electrical Bid Manager (EBM)
  • Accubid
  • ConEst
  • McCormick
  • Bluebeam
  • Other electrical estimating or takeoff platforms

Our Direction

As Omni Electrical Estimating grows, our goal is to build a team of highly skilled full-time employees capable of supporting contractors nationwide across multiple estimating platforms and project types.

We are focused on accuracy, professionalism, technology, and dependable estimating support for the electrical construction industry.
